<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Gameplay and Progression Guide">

Progression in Heave Ho 2 is structured around completing eight themed adventures. Each world introduces unique mechanics that force players to rethink how they move and interact. Unlike traditional platformers, there is no jump button; every movement is the result of gripping surfaces with your two independently controlled hands and using momentum to launch yourself or your teammates across gaps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Independent Hand Controls">
    Each trigger on your controller corresponds to a specific hand. Practice holding a surface with your left hand while rotating your body to reach for the next point with your right hand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish a Secure Anchor">
    Before the team attempts a long swing, ensure at least one player is firmly attached to a stable platform. This "anchor" player serves as the safety net for the entire human chain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build the Human Chain">
    Connect players by grabbing hands or bodies. Longer chains allow you to reach distant platforms but are much harder to stabilize and rotate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Coordinate Momentum">
    Use synchronized circular movements to build speed. The "caller" should count down (3, 2, 1, Release!) to ensure the front player is launched at the peak of the swing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Utilize Recovery Tools">
    If a player falls, the remaining team should stay anchored. Use unlockable items like the **Ima Rock** to create temporary mid-air platforms for rescued teammates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Avoid having all players release their grip at the same time. Without a fixed anchor point, the entire team will fall, forcing a restart of the current section.
</Callout>

</Section>
