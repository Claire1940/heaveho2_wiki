<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Controls and Swinging Mechanics">

The core of Heave Ho 2 lies in its "two-hand" control system. Unlike traditional platformers where you press a button to jump, here you must manually grab, rotate, and release to navigate 360-degree environments.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Individual Hand Control">
    Use the Left and Right triggers (or designated keys) to close each hand independently. Practice holding a surface with one hand while reaching for the next with the other.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Building Momentum">
    Rotate your character using the left analog stick. To cross large gaps, you must swing in a circular motion, increasing speed with each rotation before letting go.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Creating Human Chains">
    In co-op, grab a teammate's hand or body. The player at the back acts as the **Anchor**, while the player at the front makes the next grab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Perfect Release">
    Time your release at the peak of your swing's outward arc. Releasing too early or too late will result in a vertical drop or a backward launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Action | Controller Input | Pro Technique |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grip Left** | L-Trigger / L1 | Use as a pivot for rotations |
| **Grip Right** | R-Trigger / R1 | Reach for distant teammates |
| **Rotate** | Left Analog Stick | Move in rhythm with your team |
| **Throw** | Build Momentum + Release | Aim slightly above the target |
| **Catch** | Hold Grip on Contact | Open hands early before impact |

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic">
Use the **Ima Rock** unlockable item to freeze yourself in mid-air. This allows you to serve as a temporary, stationary platform for teammates who missed their jump.
</Callout>

</Section>
