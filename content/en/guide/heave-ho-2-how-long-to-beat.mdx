<Section icon={Target} title="How to Beat Heave Ho 2 Faster">

If you want to reduce the time it takes to beat the game, mastering the "Human Chain" and "Momentum Transfer" is essential. The physics engine in Heave Ho 2 is predictable once you understand how weight distribution affects your swing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Independent Grips">
    Practice moving one hand at a time. Beginners often release both triggers simultaneously, leading to unnecessary falls. Always keep one hand anchored.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sync Your Rhythm">
    When forming a human chain, have one player act as the "caller." Count out "1, 2, 3, Release!" to ensure the entire chain swings in unison for maximum distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use Support Items">
    Unlock items like the **Ima Rock** or **Arm Spray**. These tools are designed to help teams bypass difficult gaps, significantly shortening the time spent on tricky levels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize the Cat Drone">

  </Step>
</Steps>
