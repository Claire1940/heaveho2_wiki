<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Level Progression">

The demo features three distinct stages designed to teach you the fundamentals of movement and cooperation. Follow these steps to complete the demo efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Basic Swing">
    Begin by grabbing a single point with one hand. Rotate your arm using the analog stick to build momentum. At the peak of your swing, release the grab button to launch yourself forward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Implement the Hand-to-Hand Chain">
    When playing with a partner, grab their hand instead of the environment. Let the bottom player swing back and forth while the top player remains anchored to the wall.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate Moving Hazards">
    Time your swings to match the movement of rotating obstacles and moving platforms. Wait for a platform to reach its closest point before launching.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach the Goal Together">
    All players must touch the checkered goal zone to complete the stage. If one player falls, the entire team must wait for them to respawn or assist them back up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each stage introduces new environmental hazards that require quick thinking. The table below lists the primary hazards found in the demo and how to handle them.

| Hazard Type | Danger Level | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Spikes | High | Swing completely over; do not attempt to grab nearby surfaces |
| Bottomless Pits | Fatal | Use human chains to bridge wide gaps safely |
| Rotating Wheels | Medium | Grab the outer rim and release at the top of the rotation |
| Crumbling Blocks | Medium | Move quickly; these surfaces collapse after two seconds of contact |

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Alert">
Crumbling blocks will not regenerate once they fall. Plan your route beforehand to ensure all players can cross before the path disappears.
