<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Physics of Cooperation">

The heart of **heave ho 2 local multiplayer** lies in the independent control of your character's hands. Unlike traditional platformers, there is no jump button. You move by grabbing surfaces and using your body's weight to generate centrifugal force. This requires a high level of verbal communication between local players.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="The Anchor Strategy">
    One player remains firmly gripped to a safe platform while the others form a chain. This player is the safety net and should never let go until the front of the chain is secure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Human Whip">
    By swinging in a rhythmic motion, the anchor can "whip" the end of the chain across massive gaps. Timing the release at the peak of the arc is essential for maximum distance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hand-over-Hand Climbing">
    For vertical sections, alternate your grips (Left-Right-Left) while rotating the analog stick in a circular motion to scale walls efficiently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Catch Maneuver">
    If a teammate falls, an unattached player can extend an arm to catch them mid-air. This requires the falling player to keep their hands open and ready to grab.
  </Card>
