<Section icon={Shield} title="Multiplayer Survival & Progression Tips">

Succeeding in the game requires more than just fast reflexes. You must master the art of momentum, weight distribution, and team communication. Use these expert strategies to conquer the hardest cooperative levels.

### The Anchor Strategy
Before attempting any long jump or swing, always establish a designated **Anchor**. This is the player who grips a solid, non-moving part of the environment. The anchor must keep both hands firmly attached and remain completely still while the rest of the team forms a chain. Never let go of your anchor point until the front player of the chain has secured a firm grip on the next platform.

### Rhythmic Swinging
To build momentum in a human chain, all connected players must rotate their analog sticks in the same direction at the same time. Have one player act as the "caller," counting out a rhythm (e.g., "left, right, swing, release"). Releasing the grip at the peak of the swing's outward arc converts the rotational energy into maximum horizontal distance.

### Utilizing Support Items
As you complete optional challenges, you will unlock valuable support equipment that can be selected before entering a level.

- **Ima Rock**: Freezes your character in midair, turning you into a solid, temporary platform for your teammates to grab.
- **Arm Spray**: Temporarily extends your character's arm length, making distant grabs and rescues much easier.
- **Umbrella**: Allows you to float slowly downward, giving you more control over your landing trajectory.
