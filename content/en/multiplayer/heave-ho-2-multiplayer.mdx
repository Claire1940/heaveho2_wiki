<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step: Setting Up Your Multiplayer Session">

Setting up a session for **Heave Ho 2 multiplayer** is straightforward, but there are a few nuances depending on whether you are playing locally or via Steam's online servers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Multiplayer Mode">
    Launch the game and navigate to the 'Play' menu. Choose between 'Local' for couch play or 'Online' to host a private or public lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Lobby Settings">
    If hosting online, set your lobby to 'Friends Only' or 'Public'. You can also toggle 'Collision' settings and select which of the 8 worlds you want to tackle first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invite Teammates">
    For online play, use the Steam Overlay (Shift+Tab) to invite friends directly. For local play, ensure all controllers are connected and recognized by the system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Character Customization">
    Each player chooses their unique avatar and color. This is crucial for identifying yourself in the middle of a four-player human knot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Calibrate Controls">
    Before starting, have everyone test their grip buttons (L/R triggers). Physics games rely heavily on trigger sensitivity, so ensuring everyone is comfortable is key to victory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Controller Recommendation">
While keyboard-only play is an option, the developers highly recommend using a controller with analog triggers for the best **Heave Ho 2 multiplayer** experience. The precision required for swinging is much harder to achieve on a standard keyboard.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering New Multiplayer Items and Worlds">

The sequel introduces a variety of interactive items that can either help or hinder your progress. Managing these items while maintaining a grip on your teammates adds a significant layer of strategy to the **Heave Ho 2 multiplayer** loop.

| Item Name | Function | Multiplayer Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

These items appear across eight themed worlds, each with unique environmental hazards that force players to adapt their multiplayer tactics.

### The Eight Themed Worlds
1. **Weightless Space**: Gravity is low, making momentum much harder to stop.
2. **Kitchen Chaos**: Avoid hot stoves and slippery spills while navigating giant utensils.
3. **Medieval Mayhem**: Watch out for swinging maces and crumbling castle walls.
4. **Ninja Nonsense**: High-speed platforms and disappearing footholds require fast reflexes.
5. **Underwater Adventure**: Buoyancy affects your swing speed and grip strength.
6. **Construction Site**: Moving cranes and falling girders provide dynamic obstacles.
7. **Lava Land**: A classic "floor is lava" scenario where speed is mandatory.
8. **Digital Dream**: A glitchy environment where platforms can phase out of existence.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
In the **Kitchen Chaos** world, use the ketchup bottles to coat the floor near a goal in Versus mode. This makes it nearly impossible for your opponents to stick the landing, giving you an easy win.
