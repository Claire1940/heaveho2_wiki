<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer Modes: Co-op vs. Showdown">

Heave Ho 2 expands on the original's foundation by offering two distinct ways to play with your friends. Understanding the difference between the Campaign and Showdown modes is essential for choosing the right experience for your group size.

### Cooperative Campaign
In the main campaign, 2–4 players work together to navigate 72 levels across eight themed worlds. The mechanics require synchronization; if one person lets go too early, the entire chain might plummet. This mode is best for groups who enjoy puzzle-solving and "planned chaos."

### Competitive Showdown
If your group prefers rivalry, the Showdown mode features 56 levels designed for competition. Players race against each other or compete in survival challenges. The first player or team to reach five wins is crowned the victor.

<Section icon={Target} title="Setup Guide: How to Connect with Friends">

Setting up a multiplayer session in Heave Ho 2 varies slightly depending on your platform of choice. Follow these steps to ensure a stable connection for your 2–4 player group.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Mode">
    From the main menu, choose between **Local Play** or **Online Play**. If playing locally, ensure all controllers are synced to the system before starting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invite Teammates">
    In Online Play, the host can send invites via the Steam Friend List or the Nintendo Switch friend menu. On the Nintendo Switch 2, you can use **GameChat** to initiate a session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Character Customization">
    Once all players have joined the lobby, everyone can customize their characters. This is a great time to equip any **crossover skins** you have unlocked, such as the Among Us Crewmate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Choose a World">
    The host selects the starting world. For new groups, **Flying High** is recommended to learn the basic swinging and grabbing mechanics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Nintendo Switch 2 GameShare
The Nintendo Switch 2 version introduces a revolutionary GameShare feature. This allows one owner of Heave Ho 2 to share the game with up to three other nearby or online players, meaning only one person in your group needs to purchase the game to enjoy the full 4-player experience.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
When using Steam Remote Play Together, ensure the host has a high-speed wired internet connection. Since the game relies on precise physics timing, even minor latency can cause players to miss their grabs.
</Callout>
