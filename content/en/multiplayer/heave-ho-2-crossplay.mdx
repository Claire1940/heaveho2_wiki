<Section icon={Target} title="How to Set Up Online Co-op Sessions">

Setting up your first session for **heave ho 2 crossplay** or standard online play is straightforward. The game uses a simplified invite system designed for quick "drop-in, drop-out" gameplay. Follow these steps to get your team connected and ready to swing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Multiplayer Mode">
    From the main menu, navigate to the 'Online' tab. Choose 'Co-op' for the campaign or 'Showdown' for competitive versus modes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create or Join a Lobby">
    The host should select 'Create Lobby.' You can set the lobby to 'Public' or 'Friends Only.' On Switch 2, this is where you can initiate a GameShare invite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invite Teammates">
    On Steam, use the Shift+Tab overlay to invite friends directly. On Nintendo platforms, use the in-game 'Invite Friends' list or the GameChat interface for Switch 2 users.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Character Customization">
    Once all 2–4 players appear in the lobby, everyone can customize their creature. If you have unlocked crossover skins like the Among Us Crewmate or the Cult of the Lamb protagonist, equip them here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Select Your World">
    The host chooses from the 8 available worlds. If you are a beginner, it is highly recommended to start with 'Flying High' to master basic grip and swing mechanics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Connection Stability">
For the best experience in online co-op, ensure the host has a wired Ethernet connection. Physics-based games are sensitive to packet loss, which can cause "ghosting" during high-speed swings.
</Callout>
