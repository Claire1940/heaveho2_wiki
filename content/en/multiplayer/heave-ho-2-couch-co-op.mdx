<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Perfect Team Swinging">

To succeed in the campaign's most demanding levels, your team must move beyond basic climbing and master advanced swinging physics. Building momentum as a human chain is the core traversal method for crossing massive gaps. Follow these steps to execute a flawless team launch.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish a Secure Anchor">
    The designated anchor player must grab a solid, non-slippery surface with both hands. This player must hold their grip buttons continuously and avoid moving their analog stick to maintain maximum stability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Form the Human Chain">
    The second player grabs the feet or body of the anchor player. Additional players then attach themselves sequentially to form a long, hanging chain. Ensure everyone has a secure hold before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Rotational Momentum">
    All hanging players must rotate their analog sticks in unison. Start with small circular motions, gradually increasing the arc. The anchor player must remain completely still during this process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Coordinate the Countdown">
    Use a clear verbal countdown (e.g., "3, 2, 1, Release!"). The player at the end of the chain must prepare their free hand, pointing it directly toward the target platform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Launch and Catch">
    The connecting player releases their grip at the peak of the swing's outward momentum. The launched player flies across the gap and holds down their grip buttons to catch the destination ledge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Chain Position | Primary Role | Required Input | Critical Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Anchor | Hold the terrain | Continuous LT/RT hold | Never release until teammates land |
| Link | Extend the chain length | Hold teammate's body | Keep body straight to avoid tangling |
| Swinger | Build swing momentum | Synchronized stick rotation | Release at the peak of the arc |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Swing Technique">
If your team is struggling to generate enough speed, try reversing the direction of your rotation mid-swing. This sudden shift in physics can double your launching velocity, helping you clear the game's widest gaps.
</Callout>
