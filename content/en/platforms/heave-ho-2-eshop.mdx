Unlike traditional platformers, Heave Ho 2 does not feature a dedicated jump button. Movement is entirely dependent on how you manipulate your character's two elastic arms. Each hand is mapped to a specific trigger or shoulder button, requiring precise coordination to navigate the 72 campaign levels.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="The Anchor Method">
    - **Stability**: Keep one hand firmly gripped to a solid surface.
    - **Safety**: Never release your anchor until the other hand has secured a new grip.
    - **Recovery**: Use the anchor to pull falling teammates back to safety.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Momentum Building">
    - **Rotation**: Move the analog stick in circular motions to swing your body.
    - **Rhythm**: Coordinate with teammates to increase the swing arc.
    - **Release**: Let go at the peak of the arc for maximum distance and speed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Human Chains">
    - **Reach**: Connect multiple players to cross massive gaps.
    - **Order**: Place the strongest "anchor" player at the rear of the chain.
    - **Precision**: The front player must aim their free hand toward the target.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Building momentum is the core of the **heave ho 2 eshop** experience. When players work together, they can generate enough force to clear obstacles that would be impossible for a solo player. This requires verbal communication, especially during "Showdown" versus levels where speed is essential.

| Action | Controller Input | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grip Left Hand** | L / ZL Trigger | Anchor to walls, objects, or allies. |
| **Grip Right Hand** | R / ZR Trigger | Reach for the next platform or catch a friend. |
| **Rotate Body** | Left Analog Stick | Build centrifugal force for long jumps. |
