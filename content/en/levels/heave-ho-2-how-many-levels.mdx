Success in all 72 levels depends on your mastery of the unique two-handed control scheme. Unlike traditional platformers, your movement is entirely physics-driven.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish an Anchor">
    Always ensure at least one player is firmly gripping a safe surface before others attempt to swing or reach for the next objective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Coordinate Momentum">
    Communicate with your team to synchronize swings. Rotating in the same direction builds the momentum required to cross larger gaps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Safe Transfers">
    When passing a teammate or an object, release only the connecting hand once the receiver has confirmed a secure grip on the target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Support Items">
    Equip items like the Umbrella for better aerial control or Arm Spray for those tricky, long-distance reaches.
  </Step>
